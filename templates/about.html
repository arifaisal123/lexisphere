About LexiSphere

LexiSphere web app, developed by Arif Faisal, allows you to search for word meanings, synonyms, antonyms, and even translations to different languages. The app functions by using PyMultiDictionary module in the backend, which utilizes educalingo.com, synonym.com, thesaurus, and wordnet for providing its services.

The project is intended to be used for educational and non-commercial purposes only.
